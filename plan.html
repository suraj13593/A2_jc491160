Goals

    Goals of the website launching are as below:
  • Principle reason to plan the site that will encourage members to voluntary engagement in activities and performers to perform in well organized concerts.
  • New members recruitment.
  • Raise the ticket sales specially in General PUBLIC.
  • Encourage performers cooperation in media release, photo shoots, promotions etc.

Target Audience

  • Target audience of people with the specialised tastes and all ages.
  • Involment of Children is predominated by the parents or family members.

Success Evaluation

    Success can be evaluated by below points after launching the website:
  • Ticket sales statistics.
  • Increase in membership signup.
  • Accelaration in volunteers taking part in activities.
  • The number of artist taking part in activities such as photoshoots, events, media release.
